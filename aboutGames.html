  • About Micah

    Micah first began coding at Walnut Hills High School in AP Computer Science A, learning Java. He would get the opportunity to attend Miami University as an honors student, a Deans List student, and be a part of the ET Leaders program. Here, Micah would learn software such as Unity, Unreal Engine, and Game Maker Studio, as well as gain understanding of the fundamentals and philosophy of game development. Micah also received multiple game related opportunities, such as working with Undergraduate Summer Scholars Program to create Instruments of War, a 1v1 movement shooter, being the Music Chair for the Miami University Game Design Club, becoming a Teaching Assistant at Miami University for Unity, and a tutor for Unity, C# coding, and Autodesk Maya.
