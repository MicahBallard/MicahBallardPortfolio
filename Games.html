  • Sky Fishing

    You play as a bird soaring around for flying fish! Gameplay is controlled with the mouse. Move the mouse up to fly up, and down to fly down. Move the mouse left and right to strafe in those directions. Earn points and speed by catching fish, but don't fall into the water!

    Play Sky Fishing now on Itch.io!

    Accomplishments:
    - Created a game in a short span of time for a Game Jam, ranking 49th out of 94 games for the June 2024 Minigame a Month
    - coded a dynamic flight system, Menu UI, and GUI
    - 3D modeled assets in blender
    - Composed music and integrated sound effects
    - Edited gameplay trailer

    Software used: Unity, C#, Blender, Musescore 4, Audacity, Wondershare Filmora

  • Frogology

    Frogology is an open-world quest game in which players participate as a conservationist for frogs in the Sierra Nevada mountains of southern California. Throughout the game, players go on quests to locate frogs and remove threats as their populations teeter on extinction. From the first-person perspective of the game and the scientific research Frogology allows the player to change the story of the frogs in the region and change the story by making players more aware of the many species of amphibians on the chopping block of extinction due to climate change.
    This game was created during the 2024 IndieCade Climate Jam.


    Play Frogology now on Itch.io!

    Accomplishments:
    - Created a game in a month long timespan
