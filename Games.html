  • Frogology

    Frogology is an open-world quest game in which players participate as a conservationist for frogs in the Sierra Nevada mountains of southern California. Throughout the game, players go on quests to locate frogs and remove threats as their populations teeter on extinction. From the first-person perspective of the game and the scientific research Frogology allows the player to change the story of the frogs in the region and change the story by making players more aware of the many species of amphibians on the chopping block of extinction due to climate change.
    This game was created during the 2024 IndieCade Climate Jam.

    Play Frogology now on Itch.io!
